      A Five-Star Luxury Harbour-View Hotel with Seven-Michelin-Star Dining

      In the heart of Hong Kong Island, Four Seasons Hotel Hong Kong rises 45 stories above the harbour, offering world-class dining, luxurious accommodations and resort-style infinity pools. Set within the IFC complex, the five-star hotel connects directly to ifc mall, Central Station and transport links to every major district. Restaurants featuring a total of seven Michelin stars express the city’s culinary excellence, while award-winning wellness and curated cultural experiences invite you to discover the texture, energy and spirit of Hong Kong in unforgettable ways.

      Discover Hong Kong

      • Sunset Cruise & Michelin Dining

        Set sail on Hong Kong's iconic wooden junk boat. Enjoy the breathtaking Victoria Harbour sunset aboard the Aqualuna Harbour Cruise. Upon returning to the Hotel, indulge in an eight-course tasting dinner at Lung King Heen, the world's first three-Michelin-starred Chinese restaurant, curated by Chef Chan Yan Tak.

      • The Greatest Hits of HK Street Food

        4 hours
        Embark on a culinary adventure to local eateries. Indulge in dim sum, egg waffles, egg tarts, French toast, milk tea and wonton noodles. Explore specialty shops for traditional Hong Kong snacks.

      • Big Buddha and Tai O Discovery

        5 hours
        Take the private Ngong Ping 360 cable car to visit Po Lin Monastery and the majestic Big Buddha, surrounded by scenic mountains and coastlines. Explore the historic fishing village of Tai O, home to the close-knit Tanka community.
